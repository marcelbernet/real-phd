Slides 4 & 5: My Secret Weapon, Gaia

This little satellite is the hero of my thesis. It's called Gaia.

Its job is to create the most insane 3D map of the galaxy ever. It has measured the precise positions and velocities of over 1.4 BILLION stars.

Before Gaia, studying the galaxy was like trying to draw a map of Europe from a rowboat in the middle of the Atlantic. Now, it's like we have a GPS for almost every star. It's truly amazing!
